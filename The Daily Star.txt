Haji Shariatullah was a prominent leader of the Faraizi movement.
He was also a social activist and Islamic scholar of the then Bengal.
Shariatpur district in Bangladesh was named after Shariatullah in recognition 
of his outstanding contribution to social reform and anti-British movement in Bengal. 
His house in the district, however, is now in a dilapidated condition.
